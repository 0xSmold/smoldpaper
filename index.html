How to use Notes

Share secrets quickly, securely, and without a trace.
1

Impenetrable Stash

Enter your message in the "Note" tab. Military-grade AES-256 encryption happens instantly in your browser, even before it reaches the server.

2

Access Key

Set a strong password or seed phrase. Remember: SmoldPaper does not store your keys and has no access to data, so recovering a forgotten password is impossible.

3

Start Smoldering

Choose the message's lifespan. You can turn it to ashes right after the first read or set an auto-delete timer.

4

Combustion

Copy the link and send it to the recipient. In "1x" mode, the note will be permanently wiped by the server the second it's opened, or in 24 hours if unread.

How to use Stealth Chat

🕵️ Use regular messengers for an invisible invitation to true secure communication in SmoldPaper.
1

The Stash

Agree in advance with your contact on a secret seed phrase (password) to access SmoldPaper. You will need it for absolutely confidential conversations.

2

Secret Signal

Also agree on a code word for regular messengers. For example: if a message starts with "Buddy", it means the entire message containing this word is the key to the Stealth Chat.

3

The Transfer

Chat as usual. But as soon as you see the secret signal — copy the entire message. It becomes your pass to the secure room.

4

Read & Ashes

Open "Stealth Chat", paste the copied text and enter the shared password. Communicate securely: the chat burns without a trace on the server!
